FairPlay

An engaging task sharing partnership tool that brings you closer together.

Women still shoulder a disproportionate amount of the housework: the “Invisible Load”, especially mothers, who often have even less time and emotional bandwidth to deal with this burden.

FairPlay is here to help alleviate the chores, levitate above them and elevate your relationship with your partner to a new emotional level.


FairPlay is designed as a “partnership tool” rather than a “management tool”. Together with your partner, you both manage and help each other tackle everyday housework
